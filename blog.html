get in shape fast!

Posted by Mike Strong on Monday, June 6th 2021

All methods on getting in shape quickly are built on two fundamental factors: Exercise and Nutrition. Only one of the two is just not enough.

In this post, let's focus on Exercise. We will learn a 10 minutes daily routine, which is pormise to get you in shape fast! There is only one condition though: you must do it every day. After 30 days you are garenteed to see results!

You can learn on healthy nutrition to support your exercise in my previos post here .

The 10 minutes AMRAP workout routine

AMRAP stands for As Many Raps (or Rounds) As Possible. This means we use the 10 minutes time frame to repeat the routine as many times as we can. Go as fast as you can without sacrifing the technique.

As you are being consistent and doing this routine every day, you would become more fit and as a result you able to get in more rounds. You can use the same AMRAP workout as a benchmark for how far you’ve come on your fitness journey; maybe now you get 4 rounds done in 10 minutes, but a few months from now you do 5 or 6! This workout requires stamina, agility, and focus—you’ll get your heart pumping and calories burning in no time. This AMRAP contains five moves. Perform the entire circuit as many times as possible in 10 minutes; if you’re up for more, rest a minute or two and then try another 10 minutes.

The AMRAP 10 minutes routine contains 5 exercises:

  1. 5 Push-Ups
  2. 10 Squats
  3. 16 Plank Taps
  4. 20 Jumping Jacks
  5. Rest 45 second

Why should you try the AMRAP 10 minutes routine?

There are countless reasons for trying the AMRAP 10 minutes routine. Here are 5 of them:

  • It takes only 10 minutes!
  • No equipment needed
  • It is effectivly combines cardio with strength workout so you burn more calories
  • You can do it anywhere! from home, on vacation, at the office. Anywhere!
  • It is fun!

Hopefully you are conviced. Ready to get in shape? Start now!
